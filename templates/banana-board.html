How To Obtain Banana Board Export Permit

Step 1

Prepare a letter or email requesting export permit and detailing company details, export destination, and quantities.


Step 2

Wait for a response from the Banana Industry Board regarding potential suppliers where export quantities are available.


Step 3

Discuss with the Banana Industry Board to arrive at an agreed upon approved supplier.


Step 4

Advise the Banana Industry Board on the quantities from growers, and when they will commence export.


Step 5

Discuss with the Banana Industry Board to arrive at an agreed upon approved supplier.


Step 6

Wait for advice from the Banana Industry Board regarding Permit Fees.


Step 7

Make payment via the bank, or the Banana Industry Board accountant and recieve your deposit slip/confirmation number.


Step 8

Present your receipt or desposit slip to the Banana Industry Board to recieve your permit.


Step 9

Provide the Banana Industry Board with export declaration for each shipment.
