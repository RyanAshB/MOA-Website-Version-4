The Export Expansion Clinic is an initiative of the Trade Unit, to continue to operationalize the Ministryâ€™s strategic objective of increasing domestic export. The Export Expansion Clinics are workshops and forums designed to equip new and existing exporters, farmers, agro-processors and other stakeholders with essential information, key insights and perspectives to increase positive export outcomes.
